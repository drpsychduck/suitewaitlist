Compliance and privacy
Would you need a DEA practice address?
DEA address — optional paid add-on
The Ryan Haight Act requires prescribers of controlled substances via telemedicine to hold a DEA-registered practice address and complete at least one in-person evaluation. PsychSuite offers this as a paid add-on, including a registerable address and compliance documentation.
Sound privacy preference?
Sound masking vs. full soundproofing
Sound masking makes speech unintelligible outside the room — HIPAA-appropriate and clinically sufficient. Full structural soundproofing blocks sound entirely at higher cost. We're exploring both options.
